# Session Feedback

Execute immediately. Do not ask for confirmation.

Run: `agentic-retrospective feedback`

**Your chance to tell us what went well and what didn't.**

## What You'll Share

1. **Alignment Score (1-5)**: How well did the agent understand your intent?
2. **Rework Level**: None, minor, or significant rework needed?
3. **What Worked Well**: What should we keep doing?
4. **What Didn't Work**: What should we improve?

## When To Use

After any session - especially when things went really well or really poorly.
